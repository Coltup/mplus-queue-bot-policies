Mythic+ Queue Bot

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026

Mythic+ Queue Bot is a Discord bot for organizing World of Warcraft Mythic+ groups in Discord communities. This policy explains what data the bot processes and how it is used.

Information the bot collects

The bot may collect and store the following information when you use it:

  • Discord user IDs, guild IDs, channel IDs, thread IDs, message IDs, usernames, display names, and related Discord metadata needed to operate bot features.
  • Queue entries, including selected class, specialization, role, target keystone level, dungeon filters, listed keystone, queue join time, optional note text, and temporary voice channel references.
  • Registered World of Warcraft character information, including character name, realm, region, Raider.IO profile URL, thumbnail URL, Raider.IO score, active class/spec/role, faction, race, guild name, item level, ranking metadata, recent run counts, highest run, and timed dungeon counts.
  • Queue preferences saved from prior joins, such as class, spec, and role.
  • Feedback forum metadata and, when enabled, feedback message text and attachment references from the bot-created feedback forum.
  • Operational logs and interaction events, including command surface, success or error status, duration, guild/channel/user IDs, and error messages.

Information from Raider.IO

The bot uses Raider.IO to look up public World of Warcraft character information for registration, eligibility checks, queue matching, and profile display. Raider.IO data is used only for bot functionality.

How information is used

The bot uses collected information to:

  • Register player profiles and refresh public Raider.IO character data.
  • Determine queue eligibility and match Mythic+ groups.
  • Display queue status, active queue boards, private queue updates, and group-ready messages.
  • Create or reuse temporary voice channels for active queue groups.
  • Support server setup, feedback collection, troubleshooting, abuse prevention, and reliability improvements.

Message content

The bot is designed to capture feedback forum message content only when message content capture is enabled by the bot operator and the required Discord intent is enabled. Otherwise, feedback records may contain metadata without message body text. The bot does not use message content to train machine learning or AI models.

Data sharing

The bot does not sell personal information. Data is not shared with ad networks, data brokers, or unrelated monetization services. Data may be processed by Discord as part of Discord platform operation and by Raider.IO when the bot requests public character data. Bot operators may access stored data to operate, moderate, debug, or support the bot.

Data storage and retention

Bot data is stored in local JSON files controlled by the bot operator. Queue entries may expire automatically based on the configured queue time-to-live. Feedback entries and interaction events are retained up to configured storage limits. Profile data remains until replaced, removed by the operator, or no longer needed for bot operation.

Your choices

You can stop using the bot at any time. Depending on server configuration, you may be able to:

  • Use /mplus leave to remove your active queue entry.
  • Ask a server manager or bot operator to remove your profile or feedback records.
  • Remove the bot from a server if you have the required Discord permissions.
  • Change Discord privacy settings to limit direct messages from the bot.

Security

Bot operators should protect bot tokens, environment files, logs, and stored JSON data from unauthorized access. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, but access should be limited to people who need it to operate the bot.

Children

The bot is intended for Discord users who meet Discord's minimum age requirements. It is not directed to children under 13 or anyone below the minimum age required by law in their location.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the bot changes or legal, platform, or operational requirements change. Continued use of the bot after an update means the updated policy applies.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact the Discord server administrator or bot operator responsible for the server where you use Mythic+ Queue Bot.
